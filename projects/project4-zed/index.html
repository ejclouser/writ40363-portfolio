About Zed

Zed is an interactive song creator that allows you to compose music using a simple sound pad interface. Create melodies, save your compositions, and build your own music library.

How to Use:

  • Click on the sound pad buttons to create your melody
  • Enter a name for your song
  • Adjust the tempo (BPM) to your liking
  • Click "Play" to hear your creation
  • Use "Clear" to start over or "Save Song" to store your work
  • View all your saved songs in the Library section
